Artificial Intelligence for Accurate Fracture Detection: A Multi-Model Approach in Orthopedic Healthcare

This project aims to leverage artificial intelligence to enhance diagnostic accuracy in orthopedic healthcare, specifically for fracture detection across various anatomical sites beyond hip fractures. Using the FracAtlas dataset of annotated musculoskeletal radiographs, we will evaluate and compare the performance of convolutional neural networks (CNNs), recurrent neural networks (RNNs), and generative adversarial networks (GANs) in classifying, localizing, and segmenting fractures. Our approach includes data preprocessing, feature engineering, and model fine-tuning to address challenges like class imbalance and misinterpretation risks in emergency settings. The models will be assessed based on accuracy, specificity, sensitivity, and F1 score, with further analysis via ROC and precision-recall curves to mitigate bias and improve reliability. This project seeks to advance AI applications in orthopedic diagnostics, reducing diagnostic errors and supporting clinical decision-making, particularly in resource-limited or high-stress environments.

IEEE Xplore 路 Feb 18, 2023

Cognitive-Chair: AI based advanced Brain Sensing Wheelchair

This paper presents a system wherein the individual seated on a wheelchair will be given a keen headband to control the wheelchair based on neural signals or brain waves emitted from the brain utilizing technologies like Brain-Computer Interface(BCI), Electrooculography (EOG) and Electroencephalography (EEG). Subsequent to applying these technologies on a small wheelchair model, we have gotten incredible accuracy of the neural-signals. Thus the results are quite astonishing. Apart from this, in future aspects considering the safety of passengers, if anything obstructing the way of the wheelchair comes too close; causing the person to lose their concentration, the wheelchair will sense the obstacle and will apply brakes immediately. Other smart mobility features like fall-detection, ability to go through tight spaces, autonomous driving features can also be seen. The proposed wheelchair has the potential to significantly impact the healthcare business. The use of this brain-controlled wheelchair can improve a wheelchair-worthy patient's quality of life.

Springer 路 Jul 15, 2021

IoT-Based Unique Air and Noise Pollution Monitoring System

Humans around the world cause pollution but also suffer an extreme level of issues arising from the same, right from being an active contribution to the global warming crisis and to causing localized health problems to people who breathe the same air. Whereas the noise pollution side of things is slowly taking rise around the world due to the significant increase in personally owned vehicles and the shifting of heavy-duty industries into the city limits. This has an adverse role to play in the sanctity of one's mental and auditory health with certain extreme cases leading to deafness. Efforts are being made to enhance the conduciveness of the surrounding environment and reduce harmful elements by using top-notch technology and equipment. However these efforts require continuously updated databases, which is currently a field that neither possesses adequate amount of collected data nor the means to acquire the same. We propose a system which uses Internet of Things (IoT) innovation to collect the input data in real time to monitor and analyze the air quality and ambient noise. An IoT-based system operates on both automatic and manual modes for various operations like limit testing, solution suggestion procedures, etc. Emerging IoT technologies which are trending today, like IoT devices, can be used to assemble a huge amount of air and sound-associated data varying from time series data from sensors to spatial data from cameras to human observations gathered and recorded via mobile applications. In this model, the air quality sensor, the sound sensor, and temperature and humidity sensors are placed in the open surroundings, and the information gathered is transmitted to the data controller which in turn checks the obtained data against the set permissible limits and suggests necessary steps to ensure a safe and high-quality ambience.
