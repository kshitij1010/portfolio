Description

An advanced AI-driven solution for detecting, classifying, and localizing fractures in orthopedic X-rays, aimed at improving diagnostic accuracy in clinical settings.The project utilized state-of-the-art models, including ResNet for classification, U-Net for localization, and DeepLabV3+ for segmentation, achieving high precision and reliability. With a focus on enhancing real-time diagnostic support, the system offers significant potential for integration into emergency care workflows to improve patient outcomes.
